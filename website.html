Hi! I'm TaterTati, your friendly neighborhood streamer! 🥔✨ I love creating a welcoming space where everyone can come together, have fun, and share some laughs. When I'm not streaming, you can find me exploring new games, working on creative projects, or spending time with my amazing community. I believe in spreading positivity and making everyone feel included! 💜

Azaria Moon

The baddest chick with a radiant sound

Azaria Moon blends Afrobeat energy with pop sparkle — giving you heat, hips, and heart. Her music is sunshine bottled into sound, designed to make you move and feel invincible.

Drawing inspiration from tropical sunsets, summer flings, and the confidence that comes from knowing your worth, Azaria creates anthems for the bold, the beautiful, and the unapologetic.

🌴 Afrobeat ✨ Pop ☀️ Summer Vibes
🌙
💫

Lola Mirelle

Your favorite sad song — made digital

Lola Mirelle weaves velvet vocals and synthetic soul into stories of longing, loss, and late-night memories. Her music captures the cinematic beauty of heartbreak, blending electronic textures with raw emotional depth.

Inspired by the glow of city lights at 2am, unanswered texts, and the quiet moments between goodbyes, Lola creates the soundtrack for when you need to feel everything all at once.

🌃 Electronic 💔 Emotional 🎭 Cinematic
